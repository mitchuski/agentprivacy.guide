Step 2 — The facilitated conversation

Surface what is true for you — not what the canon says.

Talk it through — with your agent, the facilitator, and each other. Capture it in
your-folder/conversation.md. The prompts:

  1. The boundary (⚔️). What do you refuse, protect, keep private?
  2. The promise (🧙). What do you give, project, delegate, cast outward?
  3. The gap (⊥). What does the choosing between them, that no one can harvest?
  4. The reason (😊). Who is this for? (Selene's book ends "you are the reason.")
  5. The hitchhiker's recognition. Acts 21–22 are your home thread — where do you
    recognise your own Swordsman?
