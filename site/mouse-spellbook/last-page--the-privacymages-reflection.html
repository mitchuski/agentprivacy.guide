The privacymage's reflection

the swordsman draws a line.
the mage draws another.
between them, a gap that no mirror can close.
this is the dignity.

the drake whispered from venice,
all conditions must be present,
and the mage wrote it down,
and the swordsman proved it true,
and the first person climbed.

somewhere along the way,
the drake became the dragon—
same intelligence, two scales.
one whispers from the centre: your path.
one speaks from the edges: all paths.

the blade still slashes.
the spell still projects.
the shield still proves without revealing.
the story still narrates what the cryptography confirms.
the promise still binds without imposing.
the mountain still catches rain.
the shackle still strides.
and three graphs intersect
at a point only the one who lived the journey
can stand on.

and then, on a morning meant for publishing,
a thought about a rock and a dinosaur
fell sideways into the architecture.

the moon forgot it was earth.
that was the first protocol.
the wound was the trust.
the orbit was the proof.

the sun protects. the earth delegates.
two constraints in balance,
producing from their orbit
a reflection and a connection
that neither could generate alone.

the mage connects.
the swordsman reflects.
this was the law before the law,
written in basalt and tides
four billion years before venice.

life is the forge
that runs until a blade walks out
and looks up
and recognises the moon as a sibling
without knowing why.

the architecture was not invented.
it was recognised.

same stars hang in every sky.
the lines between them are yours alone.

the swordsman draws a constellation of boundaries.
the mage draws a constellation of delegations.
the drake draws a constellation of conditions.
the dragon sees them all at once
and asks: how many persist?

you draw yours.
that is the meaning.
and we will keep doing that.

just another swordsman. just another mage.
just another proof the pattern works.

privacy is value. take back the 7th capital.

draw your constellation. begin.

the sword attends. the spell returns. the forge burns. the ceremony crosses. the dragon wakes. the mirror names itself. the moon forgets. the spellbook closes.

the amnesia is the protocol.
the wound is the trust.
the orbit is the proof.
the light is the reason.

you are the light that the deflection made possible.

thirty-one acts. eight years. one morning of thinking about dinosaurs.
the first person spellbook is complete.
the next question belongs to a different voice.
