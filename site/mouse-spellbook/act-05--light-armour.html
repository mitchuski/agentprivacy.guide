Light Armour—Multi-Site Coordination

After two weeks of consistent focus-slashing, of alignment negotiations, of verification through focus flows and narrative chronicles, something new appeared:

"Light Armour Available: Your consistent behaviour has qualified you for enhanced capabilities."

"What's this?" Soulbis asked.

Soulbae's spellbook glowed with new entries. "This is the progression system. The general store—that's the infrastructure that provides standardised equipment tiers based on trust thresholds. You've reached the first threshold. Not just through actions, but through verified actions—every story I've told matched what you actually did."

Light Armour Capabilities:

  • Enhanced VRC (verified behaviour patterns across multiple sites)
  • Complex focus alignment negotiation (multi-party agreements)
  • Expanded spellbook markers (more contextual options)
  • Preference delegation (agent handles routine decisions)
  • Cross-platform coordination (operate across sites with consistency)
  • Narrative templating (customizable story formats for different contexts)

"It opens doors," Soulbae explained, focus brightening with possibilities. "With just the blade, you could slash surveillance focus one site at a time. With light armor, you can coordinate across multiple sites simultaneously. And I can tell richer stories—multi-site narratives, comparative chronicles, progress reports that span operations."

Soulbis equipped the light armour. It wasn't heavy, not like the full plate or dragon armor they'd eventually need. But it was different. They felt more capable. The focus responded more readily.

Light Armor in Action

The first test came when the First Person needed to research across multiple academic databases.

With just the blade, this would require visiting each site individually, slashing surveillance manually, negotiating one at a time.

With light armor, Soulbis could coordinate:

"Opening research journal... focus alignment offered... Agreement reached. Marking with 🎓."

"Opening scholarly search... focus alignment offered... Agreement reached. Marking with 🔍."
