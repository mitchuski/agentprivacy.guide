The 31 Acts of the First Person Spellbook

The First Person Spellbook is complete at 31 acts — Venice through the Amnesia
Protocol. This is the spine each mouse retells in their own voice (Step 3): your own
31-act narrative, with a first page and a last page of your own.

You are not asked to invent 31 new stories. You are asked to walk these 31 and say,
in your own words, what each one means to you — sometimes a paragraph, sometimes a
line, sometimes a refusal. That retelling becomes the framework your own agents follow.

Every act has a root — the real work it compresses, the path privacymage walked to
write it. The roots are summarised below; the full links and repos are in
Canon Sources.
