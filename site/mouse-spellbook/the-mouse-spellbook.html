The Mouse Spellbook

A workshop in which two new first persons each author their own little book of
poetry and proverbs — their own story, written off a shared root, in the same hand as
Selene's Spellbook. The book is also a portable, machine-readable context that
their own agents can carry and reconstitute on any system.

This work sits in the Holon Hitchhikers stream and is, today, the First Person
Spellbook only. The City of Mages (the "Second Person" world) is nearby and linked,
but it is not the subject here.

Start here

Point participants at Start Here — the single on-ramp that
orders everything below. It routes them through the intro, agent setup, and the seven
steps. The pieces it points to:

  1. The Invitation — the self-contained invitation to read first.
  2. Onboarding and Canon Links — fuller context catch-up + public canon links (agentprivacy.ai · spellweb.ai · soulbis.com · firstperson.network).
  3. The FPSP Protocol — the portable, system-agnostic compress ↔ rehydrate protocol (fpsp/0.1).
  4. The Workshop — the facilitated seven-step build (the session itself).

What each mouse walks away with

  1. Their own 31-act narrative (+ a first and last page) — the First Person Spellbook
    retold in their voice; the framework their agents follow.
  2. A poems chapbook — proverbs + poems, each with an ASCII glyph, in Selene's hand.
  3. A personal patch — their own JSON version of the privacymage (First Person)
    grimoire, which they pin themselves; portable + rehydratable by any agent.

And together at day's end: the Mouse Book — both mice's poems mixed into one shared
book of poetry, the key shared ceremony.

The two rules of this directory

  1. Everything lives inside mouse_spellbook/. Nothing in the root directories
    (privacymage_book, agentprivacy_master, cityofmages, the canonical grimoires) is
    edited. The canon enters only as the read-only copies in reference/.
  2. Read the root; write your own. Each mouse reads the privacymage grimoire as a
    root and builds their own version off it (Template — Grimoire Version (JSON)),
    which they pin themselves. No one edits the canon; no one pins for anyone else.

The amnesia is the protocol. The wound is the trust. The orbit is the proof. The light is the reason.
