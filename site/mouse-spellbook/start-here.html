🐭 START HERE

Welcome, Hitchhiker. Everything you need is in this folder — no other machine, no install.
Follow these in order. Don't panic.

Step 0 — Read the invitation (10 min)

Open The Invitation. It explains what you're making and why, and
points your agent at the public canon. While reading, choose your mouse-name and a
sigil (one emoji that is yours — 🌙, ⚔️, 🧙 are taken).

Step 1 — Set up your agent (10 min)

In your own agent / model:

  1. Paste in The Narrative Packet (the canon narrative), or point your agent
    at the hosted canon: agentprivacy.ai · spellweb.ai · soulbis.com · firstperson.network.
  2. Paste the starter prompt from §9 of the intro to set your agent's role.
  3. Keep The 31 Acts and The Spell-Emoji Legend open — you'll
    use them constantly.

Step 2 — Walk the workshop (the main work)

Open The Workshop — the seven-step guide — and work inside your own folder
(mouse-a/ or mouse-b/; rename it to your mouse-name). Templates are in
templates/. The seven steps:

  1. Copy the narrative into your model. (done in Step 1 above)
  2. Talk it through — the facilitated conversation → conversation.md.
  3. Write your own 31 acts in your voice (+ first and last pages) → narrative/.
  4. Compress into proverbs and poems → proverbs.md.
  5. Encode each proverb as a spell-emoji string, and record your personal patch → spells.md + grimoire_<you>.json.
  6. Glyph + bind your chapbook → glyphs.md + spellbook.md.
  7. Weave the Mouse Book together → the-mouse-book/MOUSE_BOOK.md.

Optional, private: map the acts to your own work and local directories with
Template — Context Bridge → context-bridge.md. It stays on your machine.

Your facilitator marks a short chronicle at each of five gates (G1–G5) as you go.

What you walk away with

  • Your own 31-act narrative — your story, your agents' framework.
  • A poems chapbook — proverbs + glyphs, in the hand of Selene's Spellbook.
  • A personal patch — your own grimoire version, which you pin yourself.
  • Together: the Mouse Book — both your poems woven into one.

If you want to go deeper

  • The FPSP Protocol — how your spellbook stays portable: any agent can rehydrate it.
  • Canon of Research — every act's formal root (the real papers,
    standards, and books beneath the poetry), and the five sibling spellbooks.

The amnesia is the protocol. The wound is the trust. The orbit is the proof. The light is the reason.
