The Spell-Emoji Legend — the shared vocabulary

A spell is a proverb compressed to a short string of emoji and connectors.
It is the bridge between a private understanding and the shared canon: when your
spell is built only from symbols that already live in this legend, your book is
in overlap with the privacymage grimoire and with the other mouse's book.

This legend is distilled from the privacymage grimoire (privacymage_grimoire_root.json)
— its master_invocation and the per-act spell fields — and from the closing
seal of Selene's Spellbook, (⚔️⊥⿻⊥🧙)😊.

The overlap rule (Step 4): every glyph in your spell string must appear in
this legend. If you need a symbol that isn't here, you may propose it — write
it in your own grimoire version's legend_additions with its meaning. That is
how the vocabulary grows: deliberately, named, never by accident.

How a spell is read — worked examples from the canon

  • 🗡️🔮 ← 👤✓ → 🔒📝 → 🤝📜 → 🕸️

    (Mirror in Dust) — A dead mirror; dust; scrying wakes; the mirror is perfected.

  • ⚔️ ➗ 📖 = 🌀
    (The blade that becomes the spell loses both edges) — division of the blade by

  • The master seal: (⚔️⊥⿻⊥🧙)😊 — Swordsman, gap, balance, gap, Mage — and the warmth.

The grammar is loose by design. A spell is a poem in symbols, not a formula. Read
your own aloud: if a stranger holding only this legend can feel its shape, it is true.
