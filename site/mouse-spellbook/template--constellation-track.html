6. Tracking note (for an agent)

Track this as a held constellation in the spellweb: anchor it to the First Person
Spellbook acts it lit (traced_through → the act/vertex nodes; anchors_to → the

block. Do not serialize §1–§3. The Φ-gap holds: patterns can be copied, choosing cannot
be harvested.
