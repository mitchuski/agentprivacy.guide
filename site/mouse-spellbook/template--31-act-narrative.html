Template — Your Own 31-Act Narrative

You are retelling the First Person Spellbook in your own voice. Not inventing 31 new
stories — receiving the 31 and saying what each means to you. This is your story,
and the framework your agents follow to catch up on the whole.

Work from The 31 Acts (the act index) and, when you want to go deeper,
Canon Sources (the thread down to the real work).

Three files go in your-folder/narrative/.

00-first-page.md

# <Your Book's Name> — A First Person Spellbook

<your sigil>   <your mouse-name> · Holon Hitchhiker · 2026-06-19

— First Page —

<Who are you, as a first person? What do you protect, what do you give, and who
 chooses between them? Why did you walk into this book? A few honest lines. This is
 the door your agents enter through.>

acts.md — the 31, in your voice

For each act: a paragraph, a line, or a single refusal — whatever is true. Skim the
ones that don't land; dwell on the ones that do. Mark the acts that became a proverb.
