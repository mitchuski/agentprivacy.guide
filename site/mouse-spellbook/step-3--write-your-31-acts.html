Step 3 — Write your own 31-act narrative (+ first and last pages)

Walk the 31. Say, in your own words, what each one means to you.

Using Template — 31-Act Narrative and the act index The 31 Acts,
write your own retelling into your-folder/narrative/:

  • 00-first-page.md — your opening: who you are as a first person, why you walked in.
  • acts.md — the 31 acts in your voice. A paragraph, a line, or a refusal per act —
    whatever is true. You are not inventing 31 new stories; you are receiving them.
  • 99-last-page.md — your closing: what you carry out, who it's for.

This is the heart of the day and the longest step. It is also what makes the book a real
context-catch-up framework: when your agent reads your acts.md, it learns the whole
arc the way you understand it.
