Template — the Little Book

Assemble your spellbook.md from this skeleton. It is small by design — a few pages,
in two greys and the white of the page. View in monospace so the glyphs hold.

Copy everything below into your-folder/spellbook.md and fill the brackets.

# <Your Book's Name>

<your sigil>

*a first-person spellbook*
*<your mouse-name> · 2026-06-19*


                    — Epigraph —

        <your one true thing, the sentence you would keep
         if the whole architecture vanished>


─────────────────────────────────────────────


## I. <title of proverb 1>

        <your ascii glyph here, in a fenced monospace block>

        <the proverb, one or two lines, centred in feel>

        <the poem, if you wrote one>

                                    <spell, small, beneath>
                                    polarity: <n> + / <m> −


─────────────────────────────────────────────


## II. <title of proverb 2>

        <glyph>

        <proverb>

                                    <spell>
                                    polarity: <n> + / <m> −


─────────────────────────────────────────────

  (one page per proverb — III, IV, V as you have them)


─────────────────────────────────────────────


                    — Colophon —

        just another mouse, <your-name>.            <your sigil>
        the agents kept private, the spell kept woven.
        <your reason — who this is for>

                                    (<sigil> ⊥ ⿻ ⊥ <complement>)😊

Notes on the hand:

  • Restraint. Fewer marks. White space is part of the engraving.
  • The colophon echoes Selene's: "just another mage, soulbae." / "you are the reason."
  • The seal at the end is yours — your sigil where the Swordsman/Mage sit, holding the
    gap (⊥) and the balance (⿻). If you have only one sigil, the complement can be the
    thing you set yourself against.
  • When the book is bound, copy each glyph's name into your grimoire version's
    proverbs[].glyph_ref, so the book and the machine-readable version stay in step.

(⚔️⊥⿻⊥🧙)😊
