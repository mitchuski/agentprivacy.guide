Step 1 — Copy the narrative into your model

You are the reason. The amnesia is the protocol.

In your own agent / model:

  1. Paste in The Narrative Packet (the canon narrative).
  2. Paste in reference/privacymage_grimoire_root.json (or as much as it holds), or point
    your agent at the hosted canon (agentprivacy.ai · spellweb.ai · soulbis.com — see
    Onboarding and Canon Links).
  3. Frame your agent (suggested):

    "This is the Privacymage First Person Spellbook — 31 acts about a verified first
    person who delegates to a Swordsman (privacy, the boundary, 'I will not') and a Mage
    (delegation, projection, 'I will'), with a Gap between them that does the choosing. I
    am a new first person and a Holon Hitchhiker reading this whole. Don't summarise it
    back at me — help me find what is true for me. I will retell all 31 acts in my own
    voice and compress what matters into proverbs."

→ Gate G1.

Note. The Narrative Packet is an abridged rendering; for the complete canon read all of The 31 Acts (the per-act pages are authoritative).
