Step 7 (Finale) — Weave the Mouse Book

Two sovereign first persons, one shared understanding.

Together, build the-mouse-book/MOUSE_BOOK.md from Template — Mouse Book.
This is the key ceremony of the day: you mix and match poems from both chapbooks —
yours beside the other's — arranging them so the pair makes a clearer understanding than
either alone. Where two poems rhyme in meaning (same forces, same polarity), set them
facing. Where they diverge, name the divergence — that is the overlap honestly drawn.

The Mouse Book is co-signed by both sigils. It is not anyone's patch and edits no root;
it is the proof that two first persons, each with their own book, share a vocabulary and
a gap. That shared book is the smallest Holon: two wholes, making a larger whole.

→ Gate G5 closes the day.
