📒 The Ledgers

A result exists only where the record survives. Five ledgers, one writer (the keystone — other seats return proposed entries; append-only ledgers do not survive concurrent writers, GR-10):

ledger what it is
frontier.json the sole authority for numbers (GR-1). Prose cites it; prose never restates it.
claims_register.md every load-bearing claim, tiered PROVEN / DERIVED / REPORTED / OPEN / MYTH (GR-2).
notes/KILLED_LEVERS.md negative results, filed as prominently as wins (GR-6). A killed lever is a fence, not a footnote.
chronicles/ one file per session, verdict first, handoff block at the end (GR-7). A session without a chronicle is unfinished.
SOURCES.md trace or delete (GR-9): nothing is citable unless it resolves to a concrete path.

The blank templates for all five ship in the repo's templates/ — copied by tools/new_instance.mjs with their TODOs intact, which the conformance gate then refuses until you fill them. See The Seat Contract for the config the ledgers anchor and The Ten Ground Rules for the rules they enforce.
