📐 The Model — Privacy Is Value

V(π, t) · the multiplicatively-gated privacy-value function — any single term at zero collapses V to zero.

The formal model of the agentprivacy universe — an open path we follow to discover its true form. The wiki mirror of agentprivacy.ai/model.

Abstract

agentprivacy treats privacy as a measurable, defensible value — behavioural data as the 7th capital, in a pre-property-rights phase, governed by mathematical structure rather than regulatory mandate. The framework is inscribed in two parallel registers held coherent by a shared formal core.

The mathematical core is the Privacy Is Value Model (PVM v6 · The Gathering Turn and the Moving Ceiling). It defines a multiplicatively-gated value function V(π, t) across cryptographic enforcement, credential verifiability, data quality, scope, temporal decay, holonic memory, network effects, guild coordination, reconstruction resistance, market modulation, the three sovereignty axes (Φ_agent · Φ_data · Φ_inference), and a path-integral over operational trajectories. The gating is strict: any single term at zero collapses V to zero. It commits to specific breaking conditions — non-multiplicativity of the three axes, dihedral failure, amnesia ≤ policy — making it falsifiable rather than rhetorical.

V6 names the moving ceiling. The static reading of every protective term is replaced by a ratio R(t) = (C_S(t) + C_M(t)) / H(X) — the person's entropy is fixed while adversarial capacity grows with frontier capability, so every protection carries a shelf life t* = sup{t : R(t) < 1} (C82, proven in a conditional regime). Around it sit the Existence-Leak law (C81), compositional leakage amplification (C83, capped by amnesia at Nε), the ARCH-1 bridge (C85), and obstruction-theoretic amnesia (C86 — the only term whose security is independent of t). Conjecture authority lives in the register at head C89; when any surface and the register disagree, the register wins.

The narrative registers are the grimoires. The privacymage Grimoire (🧙 First Person · v10.4) carries the original derivation across five spellbooks — First Person · Zero · Canon · Society · Plurality. The City of Mages Grimoire (🏙 Second Person · v1.8.0) carries the cast and the sixteen workshops that operate the model in practice, each Mage walking a vertex on the 64-vertex sovereignty lattice, each shop a concrete instance of one or more PVM terms.

The two registers do not collapse into each other — they strengthen each other. When a narrative act instances a math term (the same Φ_agent dihedral identity as both conjecture C14 and a forge act at V19), confidence rises on both sides. Where the math left a range the narrative now wears (C18–C21 as the Lorenz Attractor envelope), or the narrative opened a shape the math has not caught (C39 Kindred-Blade · C47 Triadic-Constraint Homology), the asymmetry is the growing edge. This is what an open path means: every claim wears its honesty, every act lists what it instances, every breaking condition is named. The model does not pretend to be closed.
