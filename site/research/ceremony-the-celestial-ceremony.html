The Progression

The ceremony operates through a progressive trust system. Each level deepens the key, increases the formal visibility, and shifts the boundary-making:

Understanding вҖ” the shared experience. Two people listen to the same poem over the same music. No technology required. The key is held between two bodies вҖ” the understanding itself is the proof. Visibility is minimal: nothing is formally disclosed, but the encounter has changed both people. The boundary is the conversation itself вҖ” what you chose to talk about, what you chose to leave silent. This is already a ceremony. This is already a key.

Constellation вҖ” the map of the understanding. Open spellweb.ai, find the nodes that match what the ceremony surfaced, trace a path through the knowledge graph. The constellation reveals your reading of the architecture вҖ” which concepts resonated, which edges you see between them. The visibility ratio increases: your choice of which nodes to include and which to exclude is the Swordsman act. Boundary-making begins here. The constellation is a spell disclosure вҖ” which domains your path touches reveals your sovereignty posture without stating it.

Blade вҖ” the forged proof. Hit evoke on the spellweb, and the constellation becomes a cryptographic artifact. The six dimensions activate based on which parts of the architecture your constellation touches. The visibility ratio inverts: the blade proves more while revealing less. The dimensions, stratum, charge level, and hash are all verifiable вҖ” but the meaning of the constellation, the poems spoken, the discussion held, the encounter that changed the territory вҖ” all private. This is where zero knowledge enters. The blade is the boundary made permanent.
