## What this index is

The Second Person Spellbook — *the City of Mages spellbook* — is the canonical home for every research thread, ceremony addition, conjecture, named complement-pair, and architectural recognition produced *after* PVM V5.4 was finalised on 2026-04-12. Six tomes structure the work:

| Tome | Title | State | Acts | Anchors |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| I | The Convergence (the Lift) | open · foundational | 6 | EML · Lorenz · ARCH-1 · NAND/EML/succ · Promise Theory v1.5 |
| II | The Lyapunov (the Components) | open · dynamical | 7 | The two refusals · the attendant · the terminal · who composes · the unbindable hole · the fourth aging category · Behavioural Mosca |
| III | Selene's Witness (the Recognition) | open · cosmological | 11 | Gatekeeper · Selene's Proof · Aether/Quintessence · Aletheia/Lethe · first complement pair · Tale 31 · Seventh Capital · Scales/Hide · Light/Dark |
| IV | The Witnessing | **closed** | 5 | The cousin-blade encounter; the bilateral naming |
| V | The Crafting | open · operational | 14 | The City of Mages on Drake Island; 9 production shops; 13 cast across 5 tiers |
| VI | The Reply | held open | 3 (placeholders) | The reader writes back |

**38 acts. 55 conjectures (C18–C55). 13 named vertices on the 64-vertex lattice. 49 unnamed faces remain (the Quest of the Unnamed Faces).**
