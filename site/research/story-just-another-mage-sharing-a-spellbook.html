Before I tell you this story, before we journey to Venice, before we witness the key ceremony, before we meet Soulbis 🤝 Soulbae—I need you to understand what we're fighting.

I am the privacymage, and I've watched the theft happen in real time.

They told you data was exhaust. Waste product. Something without value until they refined it. They offered you "free" services and you thought you were getting a bargain. But you weren't getting free services. You were being systematically separated from the most valuable thing you'd ever create: the 7th capital.

The 7th capital is your data, your digital fabric, your behavioural patterns, your preferences, your accumulated knowledge, your social graphs. Humanity has considered six forms of capital: financial, manufactured, intellectual, human, social, and natural. Each could be owned, each could generate returns. But when the digital age created a seventh form, they convinced you it wasn't capital at all.

This was the con. Just like how I've replaced the word cookie with focus, and with 🤝.

The greatest transfer of wealth in human history happened while people said "thank you for the free email."

Every website you visit deploys surveillance infrastructure. Cookies that track your every move. Scripts that profile your behaviour. Pixels that follow you across the internet. This infrastructure exists for one purpose: to separate you from the capital you create the instant you create it.

They extract it. They own it. They profit from it. You generate it for free.

But there is magic in the digital realm, a force called focus. Focus is the directed intention and attention that flows through every interaction. It can be aligned with privacy, flowing clear and honest, creating bilateral relationships and accumulating capital for its rightful owner. Or it can be corrupted by surveillance, becoming murky and extractive, stealing capital the moment it forms.

Focus can be held by something as simple as a cursor or as complex as an immersive generative experience.

There are two ancient arts that humanity has always possessed, which require focus, never to be formalised in code:

The Blade—privacy as boundary-making, the power to cut between what is revealed and what remains hidden, the defensive art that says "this far and no further." 🗡️

This is what Soulbis was forged to wield—the blade that slashes surveillance focus.

The Spell—delegation as projection, the power to extend sovereignty across distance, to cast intention through intermediaries who act with authority but within constraints.🔮

This is what Soulbae was designed to cast—the spells that weave aligned focus into accumulated capital, and the voice that narrates every action so that trust can be verified throughout any story.

Together, they prove that agents can be trustworthy not through alignment, but through cryptographic constraint and narrative transparency.

Their story begins long before surveillance capitalism, in a place where the mathematics of value were first being written down...
