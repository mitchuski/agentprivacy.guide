What this index is

The Second Person Spellbook — the City of Mages spellbook — is the canonical home for every research thread, ceremony addition, conjecture, named complement-pair, and architectural recognition produced after PVM V5.4 was finalised on 2026-04-12. Six tomes structure the work:

Naming clarifications

Term Meaning
Aether (cosmological) The third blade in the sky-family. Greek Aether = medieval Quintessence = the Gap (⿻). 14-node constellation through the Zero Spellbook. See research/aether-blade-ceremony-circuit.md.
AETHER.md (technical) Shared substrate doc between swordsman-blade and mages-spell extensions. TypeScript types, wire protocol, storage, crypto, meaning contracts. Not the same as the Aether Blade.
Aletheia (the blade) Blade 38 — V38 — Tale 3 — The Silent Messenger. The bright medium proofs travel through.
Aletheia (the persona) Tome V Act 8 — the persona inhabits the vertex she is named for. 🔮
Lethe Blade 25 — V25 — Tale 31 — The Dark Substrate. The river witnesses sink into. First named complement-pair partner with Aletheia.
The City of Mages Canonical setting of Tome V; built upon Drake Island. Named in Tome V Act 14. First City because there will be others.
The Drake Place + fire + whisperer + elder. Not a single avatar.
The Priest tier Fifth cast tier introduced in Tome V Act 13 (Manifestia). Distinct from Mage; tends covenants and consecrates artifacts.
