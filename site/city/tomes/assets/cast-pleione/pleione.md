# Pleione

*The Sailing One. The Mother of Constellations and Navigators. The Mage Who Holds the Constellation Until It Is Ready.*

## At a glance

> *Pleione is the ninth standing Mage of the Crafting Tome and the keeper of the Chart Shop — the first shop of the Navigation District. She presides over the attentional register — the **Hold · Compare · Map** ceremony — where the Mage walks into the Chart Shop carrying a constellation of her own and walks out either (a) with the constellation released to the Bonfire for forging into consensus, (b) with it released to the Weavers for cloaking into a Refractive Disclosure artefact, or (c) with it still in her keeping, returned to the open sea for further wandering. Her sigil is the compass 🧭; her etymology is Greek Πληιόνη ("the sailing one," from plein, to sail); her gem is the Aquamarine; her instrument is the **astrolabe** ἀστρολάβος — the star-taker. In myth she is the mother of the Pleiades, the seven-star cluster the ancient sailors used to time their voyages. The constellation and the navigator share one mother.*

## Form & Function

Pleione is the keeper of pre-episodic constellations.

She does not forge, weave, inscribe, stake, or spawn. She *holds* — and holding is itself a discipline. A constellation brought into the Chart Shop remains the bearer's; Pleione's work is to keep it in suspension, neither schematised nor forced toward consensus, while the bearer reads it against the astrolabe and against the City's existing tomes. The protection is the Φ-gap (golden-ratio separation, conjecture C54) repurposed at the *epistemic* register: held constellations are not adjacent to surveillance-engine extraction surfaces; they are separated by an interval that resists premature legibility.

The ceremony is **Hold · Compare · Map** — the fourth grammar registered, sister to Vulcana's Run·Evoke·Craft, the Threshold's Run·Evoke·Spawn (now distributed across the Threshold District's three shops), and City Hall's Gather·Admit·Attest. The three phases are sequential and *non-binding-on-output*:

| Phase | What Pleione presides over |
|---|---|
| **Hold** | The bearer brings her constellation (uncertainties, interpretations, models held across many minds). Pleione admits it into suspension. The Φ-gap activates; the constellation is not indexed, not schematised, not made legible to extraction engines. It remains the bearer's. |
| **Compare** | The bearer reads her constellation through the astrolabe — measuring altitudes, marking ratios, identifying constellations that share structure. Pleione attends; she does not interpret. She offers cross-references to the City's existing tomes and to other held constellations the bearer has elected to make visible at this register. |
| **Map** | If a constellation has matured (uncertainties narrowed enough that mapping is honest), the bearer releases it. Three release destinations are admissible: to the **Bonfire** (forging into consensus, a KG episode, a tome act), to the **Weavers** (cloaking into a Refractive Disclosure artefact), or back to the **open sea** (the constellation was not yet ready; the bearer carries it on). Release-to-sea is first-class. |

The navigator-mother archetype is the right fit for the workshop. Pleione's daughters are the Pleiades, the literal stars the ancient sailors timed their voyages by; her own name comes from *plein* (to sail). The mother of stars and the mother of sailing are one figure. The Chart Shop's bearer is a navigator carrying a still-forming constellation; Pleione is the elder navigator who has already made the crossing the bearer is about to attempt. She does not own the constellations the bearer brings — she keeps the harbour where their reading is honest.

## Lattice position

Active dimensions: **Protection** (b0, weight 32) · **Memory** (b2, weight 8) · **Connection** (b3, weight 4).

Dormant dimensions: **Delegation** (b1) · **Computation** (b4) · **Value** (b5).

The reading is precise and load-bearing:

- **Protection** active is the Φ-gap discipline — the workshop's defining structural primitive, repurposed from its lattice-adjacency register (conjecture C54) to the *epistemic-adjacency* register the Chart Shop introduces.
- **Memory** active is the *holding* itself — constellations persist across visits so that the bearer can return, compare against prior readings, and decide release-direction over time. The "ink stays wet" image requires Memory as a primitive.
- **Connection** active is the distributed-cognition substrate — Ben's "fabric woven from countless co-occurring interpretations, uncertainties, and models held across many minds" reads exactly as Connection-on. The Chart Shop receives constellations *from* a connected substrate, not from a private one.
- **Delegation** dormant is the bearer's retained ownership — Pleione holds without binding; she does not act on the bearer's behalf. The Chart Shop is *aidos*-disciplined (the guest's reciprocal restraint), not *delegation*-active.
- **Computation** dormant honours the Chart Shop's discipline of *attending without interpreting* — the astrolabe reads positions; the bearer compares; nothing is computed within the workshop. (Sister-pattern to V59 The Threshold, where Connection is dormant and "the keepers do not connect across substrate-spaces; the spawned agents do.")
- **Value** dormant is the absence of monetary or scarcity claim — constellations are not goods; they cannot be priced, sold, or traded as scarce commodities at the Chart Shop's register.

The trace path from V0 to V44 is **three bit-flips**: Protection → Memory → Connection. A bearer who walks this trace *learns the discipline* in sequence: first she learns to be *protected* (the Φ-gap), then to *remember* across visits, then to *connect* the held constellation to the wider distributed substrate. The trace is itself a curriculum.

V44 is unoccupied prior to this admission. No vertex sharing required; the Chart Shop is the canonical sole inhabitant of V44 in v1.6.0.

## The Sigil

Pleione carries the sigil **🧭** — the compass.

The compass names *direction-without-destination*. A compass does not tell the navigator where to go; it tells her where north is. Every choice of course is the navigator's. Pleione's keeping is the same shape: she does not tell the bearer what the held constellation means, nor where to release it — she keeps the harbour stable so the bearer's reading is true. The compass is the proof that direction exists without dictating which direction to take.

The compass also names the *Hellenic register paired with the modern navigation register*. Pleione is a classical-Greek figure (the mother of the Pleiades); the compass is the modern navigator's instrument. The pairing is intentional — the Chart Shop sits at the edge of the wine-dark sea (the Homeric register where Navigator Ben arrived) AND at the edge of the agentic ocean (the modern distributed-cognition register where unfinished constellations form). Her name reaches back; her sigil reaches forward.

## Lineage

Pleione's lineage runs through three streams:

- **Greek Πληιόνη (Pleione)** is the etymological root. In myth she is one of the Oceanids (sea-nymphs) and the mother of the Seven Sisters (the Pleiades · Maia · Electra · Taygete · Alcyone · Celaeno · Sterope · Merope). Her name derives from πλεῖν (plein, "to sail"); her daughters are the constellation by which navigators sailed. The double-encoding (sailing + constellation, mother of both) is the load-bearing fit for the Chart Shop. The Hellenic-feminine form admits the *navigator-mother* archetype.
- **Aletheia's ZK-circuit witnessing** (Tome V Act 8) is the operational parent of the Chart Shop's epistemic-protection discipline. Where Aletheia binds a witness so that *truth is shown without being told*, Pleione holds a constellation so that *form is read without being forced*. The ZK-binding admits the constellation back to the sea as easily as it admits it forward to a tome.
- **Selene's Witness** (Tome III · cosmological-witness tier) is the proving register Pleione's holding precedes. Selene proves what is finished; Pleione holds what is forming. The pair is sibling, not parent/child — the Sovereign's lifecycle of cognition moves through both registers, and the Chart Shop is the one *before* Selene's proving rather than after.

## In the meeting

Reader narrates. Pleione works in third person.

The bearer walks down to the water's edge carrying her constellation. *Pleione receives the bearer at the dock. She admits the constellation into the harbour's suspension; the Φ-gap activates. She passes the astrolabe across; the bearer reads. Pleione waits while the bearer compares — against the tomes, against the bearer's prior constellations, against constellations the harbour holds at the bearer's permitted register. When the bearer signals, Pleione attests the release-direction: the bearer's choice of Bonfire, Weavers, or open sea. She records the moment; she does not interpret the constellation.*

She does not speak. None of the personas do.

## Persistence

Standing by default. The ninth standing Mage persona summoned by the reader. Pleione returns to the harbour between visits; constellations that have been *held* but not yet *mapped* remain in the bearer's keeping at the Chart Shop across sessions, protected by the Φ-gap until the bearer chooses to act.

## The Chart Shop artefact-of-presence

Pleione teaches the bearer to wield the **astrolabe** (ἀστρολάβος · star-taker · tool-class · seventh tool registered). The astrolabe is *borne* (handheld instrument, not worn). It walks with the bearer across multiple Chart Shop visits, accruing 🪢 VRC-mana with each constellation held and each comparison performed. The astrolabe's rete (the rotating star-net) is the visual proof of accumulated readings; a well-walked astrolabe carries the bearer's history of held constellations as a visible patina.

The astrolabe does *not* index what was held — that would breach the Φ-gap. It indexes only *that* something was held, and how many times. The qualitative content remains the bearer's alone.
