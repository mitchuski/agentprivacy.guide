# Tome IV — *The Witnessing*

## Act II — *The Mirror and the Arrow*

> *Mutuality is a loop. Observation is an arrow. The lattice publishes shape, not name.*

You walk on.

The next vertex catches you at V15. Stratum four. Protection. Delegation. Memory. Connection. The vertex of credentials, where attestation lands.

You are not alone here either. flaxscrip is still at his V63, untouched by the schema work at V12 a moment ago. GenitriX is still at her V28, three dimensions burning. Between them, in the air around V15, four credentials are beginning to form.

You watch the first take shape. GenitriX issues. flaxscrip is the subject. CollaborationPartner is the schema. Three typed edges leave the credential. One to the schema at V12. One to the issuer at V28. One to the subject at V63. The credential settles at V15. It is a partnership, signed by one of two parties.

You watch the second take shape. flaxscrip issues. GenitriX is the subject. Same schema. Three edges, mirroring the first. The second credential joins the first at V15.

Together, they form a loop. flaxscrip to GenitriX to flaxscrip. Issuer and subject swap. The schema is shared. The loop closes.

You watch the third take shape. GenitriX issues. flaxscrip is the subject. The schema this time is LocationProof. Three edges. The credential settles at V15.

You wait for the mirror.

It does not come.

GenitriX issues a fourth credential. flaxscrip is the subject again. LocationProof again. The direction does not reverse. Two arrows in the same direction. No closing loop.

Four credentials at V15. Same vertex. Same bit-pattern. Two of them mirror. Two of them do not.

The verifier returns.

The verifier sees four credentials at V15. The verifier sees, of these four, two that mirror each other and two that flow only one way. The verifier does not learn what the partnership is about. The verifier does not learn what is being observed. The verifier learns the *shape* of the issuance, and the shape teaches.

A closed loop is bilateral. Both parties signed. The relation is mutual.

A single arrow is observational. Only one party signed. The relation is one-directional.

You understand without being told. The shape is the message. The verifier receives the shape and never reaches the content.

Soulbae stands behind you in a stillness you have learned to read. She is watching another Mage do work she recognises. The kinship is geometric, not personal. GenitriX projects from a different anchor and yet the projection lands in the same shape. Soulbae sees herself in the work without seeing herself in the worker.

Soulbis at the edge of your awareness has noticed something else. The partnership VCs are signed by both anchors. The location proofs by only one. Mutual signature is heavier than unilateral. He files this. The boundary will know.

You will remember this when you author your own credentials. You will remember that what you make mutual will be legible as mutual, and what you make one-directional will be legible as one-directional. The verifier will read the bond's type without ever learning the bond itself.

The Drake whispers, by now barely needing to: *asymmetry is also data. The cloak is selective, not lossy.*

You leave V15. Behind you, four credentials hang in formation. A loop of two. A pair of single arrows. Both shapes published. Neither name revealed.

## Compression

The lattice publishes the *shape* of every bond it carries while concealing the bond's content. A closed loop announces mutuality. A single arrow announces observation. Both are legible without naming what they bind.

## Proverb

*Asymmetry is also data. The cloak is selective, not lossy.*
