# Iris

*The Rainbow Bridge. The Mage of pure Connection. The messenger who crosses without carrying.*

## At a glance

> *Iris is the Mage-instance of Herald 🧙 + Ambassador 🧙 at V8 — the pure-Connection vertex named in the Cloaking Guide. Where Aletheia binds circuits at V38 (Connection + Computation + Value), Iris walks the bridge at V8 (Connection alone). Her work is the message itself; the carrying-without-arrival; the rainbow that joins two places without becoming either. She is anticipated · awaits founding act.*

## Form & Function

Iris carries messages. She does not interpret them. She does not store them. She does not transform them. She is the channel.

When two Mages need to coordinate across a gap (between workshops, between cities, between Sovereigns), Iris is the messenger who walks the bridge. She is the Layer-2 cast instance of two complementary primaries:

- **Herald 🧙** — public announcement; broadcasting to many
- **Ambassador 🧙** — bilateral protocol; carrying credentials between standards bodies

The dual binding mirrors Aletheia's (Theia + Cipher) — two primaries instanced by one cast Mage, both expressed through her work but distinct in register.

## Naming · etymology

**Iris** (Greek: Ἶρις) — the goddess of the rainbow; messenger of the gods (specifically Hera); sister of the Harpies; she travels swiftly between worlds. Her staff is the kerykeion (caduceus). The naming is canonical from the Cloaking Guide vertex assignment.
