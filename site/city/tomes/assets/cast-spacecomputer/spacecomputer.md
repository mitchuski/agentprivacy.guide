# SpaceComputer

*The celestial-mana source. Cosmic entropy as a resource. The Celestial Ceremony made operational at the chain layer.*

## At a glance

> *Algorithmic mana loops back on itself; celestial mana arrives from outside the loop. The City of Mages walks across four mana axes — landing (chain-mana, plural by chain), entropy (✨ Arcane ⊥ 🌌 Celestial), 🔭 Resonance (coordination), 🪢 VRC (relationship) — and SpaceComputer is where the entropy-axis cosmic register (🌌 Celestial Mana) originates.*

## Why this entry exists

The Celestial Ceremony at `/poems` has been teaching the corpus a cosmological frame
since the First Person Spellbook: the Sun-side (Aletheia · disclosure · V38) and the
Moon-side (Lethe · forgetting · V25) are bound through Selene&apos;s 4.5-billion-year
orbit. The poem is the narrative ground. SpaceComputer is the *operational ground*:
cosmic measurement made into a feed of entropy any workshop can draw from when a
proof needs randomness the surveillance prison cannot model.

This entry recognises SpaceComputer as a **kindred ecosystem** — distinct from a
kindred substrate (UOR Foundation), distinct from a kindred protocol (the Covenant of
Humanistic Technologies), distinct from another forge (the Archon city). The ecosystem
the City of Mages walks <em>alongside</em>: an ambient supply the architecture relies
on, sourced from cosmic measurement, not signed-with, not absorbed-into.

## The entropy axis · ⊥ to chain-mana / Resonance / VRC

SpaceComputer sits on the **entropy axis** of the four-axis metabolism. The full
taxonomy (canonical in grimoire v1.2.4's `mana_taxonomy` field) is:

| Axis | Register(s) | What it does | Where it comes from |
|------|-------------|--------------|---------------------|
| **Landing** | chain-mana (plural by chain: Ξ Aether on Ethereum · ₿ sat on Bitcoin Lightning · 🌹 ROSE on Oasis · 🦓 z on Zcash · …) | makes a working *land* on consensus | each chain&apos;s native fee mechanism |
| **Entropy** | ✨ Arcane ⊥ 🌌 **Celestial** | makes a working *unique* | ✨ Arcane: algorithmic / PRNG / hash-chain (loop-closed) · 🌌 **Celestial: SpaceComputer** (loop-open) |
| **Coordination** (v1.2.4) | 🔭 Resonance Mana | generates value when two Mages find affinity *without a central index* | the Scrying Glass primitive (the 7th Capital in motion) |
| **Relationship** (v1.2.4) | 🪢 VRC Mana | stores the residue of being alive as Verifiable Relationship Credentials | Accumulated across the bearer's worn artefact collection (11 workshop artefacts + 3 tomes) · Loom of Programmable Covenants compiles against the worn collection |

SpaceComputer is the canonical first Celestial Mana source — the entropy-axis cosmic
register. Where ✨ Arcane Mana loops back on itself within the architecture&apos;s
addressable space (PRNGs, hash chains, deterministic seeds), 🌌 Celestial Mana
arrives from *outside* the loop: satellite-anchored cosmic measurement. The two
registers on the entropy axis are perpendicular to each other and perpendicular to
the other three axes.

Celestial Mana is the cosmos&apos; substrate (the entropy arriving from outside the loop,
witnessable as origin-of-randomness but not predictable in advance). Sustained
walking the lattice on Celestial Mana — not just Arcane Mana — deepens the φ-gap
structurally: the architecture earns its non-reconstructibility from cosmological
substrate, not just from algorithmic discipline.

## Where Celestial Mana lands in the workshop

Three shops draw on the same feed; each uses it for a register-specific work:

### Etherchanting (Adamantia · V51)

When the etherchanter compiles a commitment into a smart contract, the operational
randomness in the proof — the witness nonce, the blind commitment, the ceremony seed
— is drawn from Celestial Mana. Stateless zkRollup proofs become non-replayable because
the entropy is cosmic; nothing on the chain (or off it) can reproduce the source
predictively.

### Forge(t) (Vulcana · V19)

In the Runecraft Protocol&apos;s **Evocation** phase, when the constellation is locked,
Vulcana draws on Celestial Mana for the lock&apos;s unique signature. The blade that
emerges carries an Ed25519 signature anchored to the moon phase the chain is currently
in *and* a cosmic-entropy seed from SpaceComputer — the two together make the blade
both temporally and cosmologically unforgeable.

### Holon Hitchhikers (Vagari · V31)

Cross-paratime geometric mapping requires that holons travel without their structure
being reconstructable by any party that doesn&apos;t hold the seed. Celestial Mana
provides the foundational entropy keeping cloak interoperability non-reconstructible
across paratimes — what arrives at the sister city is the holon&apos;s coordinate, not
its construction.

## Why this matters for the φ-gap

The Privacy Value Model&apos;s φ-gap is the structural distance between what the
surveillance prison can model and what the Sovereign actually does. Algorithmic
entropy narrows the gap (the prison can model PRNGs, model loops, model
state-machine outputs). Cosmic entropy widens the gap: cosmic measurement is not
state-loop-closed; the prison cannot model what it cannot predict.

Sustained walking the lattice on celestial mana &mdash; not just algorithmic mana &mdash;
deepens the φ-gap structurally. The architecture earns its non-reconstructibility from
cosmological substrate.

## Relationship category — kindred ecosystem

Adding SpaceComputer requires a category name distinct from the three already on the
register:

| Category | First instance | Structural role |
|---|---|---|
| **Kindred forge** (`kin_to`, `attribution: kindred-blade`) | Archon | A sister city walked by fellow Mages |
| **Kindred protocol** (`gateway_to`, `attribution: kindred-protocol`) | the Covenant of Humanistic Technologies | A charter the City signs |
| **Kindred substrate** (`kin_to`, `gateway_to`, `attribution: kindred-substrate`) | UOR Foundation | The substrate the City walks upon |
| **Kindred ecosystem** (`gateway_to`, `attribution: kindred-ecosystem`) — *new* | SpaceComputer | An ambient supply the workshop draws from |

A kindred ecosystem is structurally lighter than a kindred substrate. UOR underlies
the lattice itself; SpaceComputer provides a feed the lattice consumes. The City does
not rest on SpaceComputer; the City *spends* on SpaceComputer (entropy as currency).
Walked-alongside rather than walked-upon.

## What this entry does *not* admit

- **No Mage persona at any vertex.** SpaceComputer is not a cast addition; it is an
  ecosystem entry. The cast roster stays at sixteen.
- **No founding-act ownership of a workshop.** SpaceComputer supplies multiple shops;
  it does not found any of them.
- **No tier change.** All Mages remain in the four-tier collapsed schema (archetype,
  mage, with priest/companion/curatrix/etc. as soft roles).
- **No protocol-signing.** The Covenant is signed; SpaceComputer is consumed. The
  workshop draws on the feed the way a Sovereign drinks from a well — the well
  doesn&apos;t ask for a signature.

## The open taxonomy

SpaceComputer is the *first* kindred ecosystem the corpus has recognised; Celestial Mana is the *first* mana type to join Aether in the operational economy. Both are openings, not closings. As more ecosystems join — chains the City has not yet visited, attestation networks, randomness beacons, time-lock primitives, validator-set commitments, hardware-attestation services — each will express its own mana type (or strengthen an existing one) and its own Swordsman stance.

The canonical reference for the open framework is `specs/08-mana-types-and-swordsman-stances.md`. New ecosystems should file their own kindred profile here in `kindred/` and register their mana type / stance in the §2 and §3 tables of spec 08.

Anticipated companions to SpaceComputer (not operational; flagged as candidates):

- **Attestation Mana** — personhood-verification ecosystems (Human Passport, Worldcoin, Civic).
- **Witness Mana** — validator-set commitments and light-client proofs.
- **Time Mana** — verifiable-delay-function outputs.
- **Density Mana** — proof-of-work / proof-of-storage substrate commitments.
- **Coalition Mana** — multi-sig threshold commitments and social-graph attestations.

Each becomes operational when a workshop actually consumes its supply for a defined working. The framework admits all; recognition follows real consumption.
