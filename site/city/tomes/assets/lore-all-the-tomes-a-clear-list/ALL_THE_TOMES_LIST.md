# All the Tomes — A Clear List

## §1. The Spellbooks

The agentprivacy corpus is organised into **Spellbooks**. Each Spellbook holds Tomes. Each Tome holds Acts. Each Act narrates one operational moment of the architecture.

There are currently three Spellbooks recognised:

| Spellbook | Status | Address | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| **First Person Spellbook** | Closed | privacymage's canonical corpus | The Sovereign's interior architecture; dual-agent split; the Drake's elder voice |
| **Second Person Spellbook** | Open | agentprivacy-docs (the bound collection) | The reader as cousin Sovereign; cousin-blade work; the City of Mages |
| **Zero Spellbook** | Anticipated | Not yet opened | The zero-knowledge register; structured around verification primitives |

Other Spellbooks are anticipated — Society, Plurality, Canon — but have not been formally opened.

## §2. Tomes of the First Person Spellbook (closed)

The First Person Spellbook closed in early 2026 after twenty-eight acts narrating the dual-agent architecture. The closing is structural — the Sovereign's interior architecture has been canonically rendered. The clearing made room for the Tomes that followed.

**Notable acts referenced from the Second Person Spellbook**:

- Act XII (*Lethe*) — substrate of forgetting; underpins Vulcana's Forge(t) wordplay and Memora's selective inscription discipline
- Acts XV-XX (*Wound and Cap*) — finite resources backing infinite claims; underpins Custos's stakes and Lampyra's heartbeats
- Act XXIV (*The Holographic Bound*) — the Oasis Protocol; underpins Vagari's Holon Hitchhikers and the cross-city travel pattern
- Act XXVII (*The Forge*) — canonical forging primitive; lineage for Vulcana's Runecraft Protocol
- Acts I-VII (*The Dual-Agent Split*) — Soulbis ⊥ Soulbae; the architectural ground for the entire corpus

The First Person Spellbook is held privately by privacymage as the canonical record. Public references to its acts are made from the Second Person Spellbook with proper attribution.
