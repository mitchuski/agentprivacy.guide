## Ceremony Shape — ADMIT · E · READ · E · ATTEST · E · SHIFT
- **ADMIT.** Stand at the shop. The alexandrite reads your archetype-light; Hermaion appears in the matching aspect.
- **READ.** Hermaion opens the bestiary; the candidate framework's provenance, license, and stewardship are surfaced.
- **ATTEST.** Caducea ☤ performs the fitting under bilateral consent; Hermaion attests the registry-truth.
- **SHIFT.** The artefact-class is announced (caduceus-staff · Mage; herald-sentinel · Swordsman); the bestiary's admission-count increments by one.
