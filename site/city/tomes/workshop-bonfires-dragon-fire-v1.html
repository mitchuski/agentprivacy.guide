Ceremony Shape — Gather-and-Witness

  • Gather. Walk the nine waypoints. Socrat0x will not rush you. The orbs settle close to the flame; charge accumulates slowly.
  • Witness. Lap the constellation. Cast at least one question (mark) from below. The fire records the question; the graph remembers.

The Bonfire forges a witness blade rather than a commissioned blade — the proof is that you stood at the fire and were seen, not that you commissioned an artefact. Witness blades render as round with ember glow in the spellweb.
