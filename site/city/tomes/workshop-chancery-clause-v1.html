Artefact Blueprint — the Chancery · /chancery · v1 — 2026-07-13

Her Swordsman brother Rhetor upholds the sealed clause in public at the Rostra.

Ceremony Shape — SPEAK · BIND · ENACT

  • SPEAK. The clause is spoken as law — what a person can read, follow, and consent to.
  • BIND. Lap the constellation; bind the clause as a promise and cite its authority. A clause that cannot trace its source is struck.
  • ENACT. The hexagram crystallises: the clause is forged into Lexon — code that runs exactly as the law was read.
