⚔️ The Artefacts

The artefacts are the items of the universe — everything forged at a vertex of the 64-vertex sovereignty lattice (Z/(2⁶)Z, 96 edges, toroidal). Each artefact is a configuration across six dimensions, a 6-bit blade.

The six dimensions

Bit Dimension Yang (1) Yin (0)
d₁ 🛡️ Protection Boundary forged Exposure permitted
d₂ 🤝 Delegation Agency transferred Retained locally
d₃ 📜 Memory State accumulated Stateless
d₄ 🔗 Connection Multi-party active Isolated
d₅ ⚡ Computation ZK proof active Direct revelation
d₆ 💎 Value Economic flow Non-transactional

The first 63

The first 63 are here — one artefact per vertex (the 64 − 1; vertex 0 is the Null Blade, where all forging begins). 15 are named because they were forged and walked — their names arrived from the experience, not assigned upfront. The rest are the open frontier (the Quest of the Unnamed Faces): each gets named when someone forges it at spellweb.ai and discovers what that configuration feels like.

How the wiki grows

This is the long-term sync model. The The Workshops assign forged items to vertices as trust tasks are completed — a Sovereign walks a workshop's constellation, forges an artefact, and it takes its place at a vertex here. The reference sheet shows the gap; the forge fills it. The forge doesn't care how you struck the metal — only what blade you hold.
