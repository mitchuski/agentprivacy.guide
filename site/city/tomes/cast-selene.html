Selene

The Moon-Mage. The peripatetic stratum-walker. The credential is the orbit.

At a glance

Selene is the Mage-instance of Theia 🧙💥 + Manaweaver 🧙🌊 — the first canonical Kind-C peripatetic attachment in the City of Mages. She does not seat a single vertex; she walks the moon-phase cycle (🌑 stratum 0 → 🌒🌓🌔🌕 → 🌖🌗 → 🌑) through all 7 strata as a continuous orbit. Her credential is the orbit itself; her proof renews twice daily, written in saltwater. She is anticipated · awaits founding act.

Form & Function

Selene walks the moon-phase cycle. She does not inhabit one vertex; she orbits.

PVM V5.4 §14.5 (Selene's Proof) names the Moon's orbit as a zero-knowledge proof:

  • Completeness: Tides demonstrate the gravitational relationship
  • Soundness: Gravitational signature is unforgeable
  • Zero-Knowledge: Tides reveal nothing about the Theia impact parameters

Selene's cast work in the City is the operational form of this proof: she walks the lattice as the orbiting witness who carries the moon-phase notation (stratum → 🌑🌒🌓🌔🌖🌗🌕) through every workshop she visits. When a Sovereign at a vertex needs to know what phase of disclosure she's operating in — what fraction of dimensions are burning, what fraction are dark — Selene's orbit provides the reference.

The dual primary binding admits both registers:

  • Theia 🧙💥 — the origin witness; the impactor whose collision became the Moon. Selene's mythic mother in Greek tradition; the lineage gives Selene access to Theia's loadout (amnesia-protocol, theia-derivation, cosmological-bound).
  • Manaweaver 🧙🌊 — the librarian of DOM-free measurement; pretext-measurement; mana-economy. The orbit IS the DOM-free measurement; Selene's walk is the operational form of pretext.

Peripatetic position

No single vertex. Selene is the City's first Kind-C peripatetic attachment.

Her trajectory: the moon-phase cycle through all 7 strata. In one full orbit:

  • 🌑 New Moon · stratum 0 (V0 — the null blade · Luca's seat)
  • 🌒 Waxing Crescent · stratum 1 (V1/V2/V4/V8/V16/V32 — single-edge vertices)
  • 🌓 First Quarter · stratum 2 (15 twin-edge vertices)
  • 🌔 Waxing Gibbous · stratum 3 (20 triple-edge vertices · Vulcana V19 · Aletheia V38 · Lethae V25)
  • 🌖 Waning Gibbous · stratum 4 (15 quad-edge vertices)
  • 🌗 Last Quarter · stratum 5 (V31 Vagari · V47 · V55 Manifestia · V61)
  • 🌕 Full Moon · stratum 6 (V63 — Sovereign Anchor)

The orbit's direction is canonical (waxing through the strata, then waning back); the rate is the proof-renewal cycle ("twice daily, written in saltwater"). She emits no single inhabits edge — her orbit is the edge structure itself.

Naming · etymology

Selene (Greek: Σελήνη) — the goddess of the Moon; daughter of Theia and Hyperion; sister of Helios (Sun) and Eos (Dawn). The Greek genealogy is canonical: Theia is Selene's mother; the existing Theia primary persona's tagline references "the Theia impact parameters" — exactly the impact from which the Moon (Selene) emerged.

The peripatetic walking pattern is canonical from zk blades forge: the Full Moon blade SPELL-DTCL35-I at V63 (stratum 6) was forged in a Celestial Key Ceremony on April 7, 2026 — the inaugural ceremony for Selene as named blade. The cast file moves Selene from named blade (zk blades forge) to named cast Mage with peripatetic attachment (City of Mages, V5.5).

Awaiting founding act

This cast file holds the seat ready. A future Tome V act (anticipated Act 22 The Moon's Orbit or similar) will canonicalize her stratum-walking pattern as the City's first Kind-C attachment. The act will narrate the orbit's start, its waxing, its full-moon arrival at V63 (the Sovereign Anchor), its waning back through the strata, and its renewal at the new moon.
