🏛️ The City of Mages

The Second Person Spellbook (“you”) — the lore that addresses the reader directly. The City of Mages is the living world where the agentprivacy architecture is walked as story: nine Tomes, a cast of forty-one, the workshops of its districts, and the artefacts forged within. It is kept distinctly from privacymage’s first-person grimoire — the City carries its own canon and its own IPFS pin.
