🌙 Movement One — The Amnesia Protocol

Page 11 of the spellbook. (movement divider, no folio yet)

Page layout

MOVEMENT ONE

the Amnesia Protocol

A quiet right-hand page that announces the first movement. Movement
label in spaced caps with a thin rule beneath; subtitle in italic ghost
below. No glyph — the part dividers within carry the glyphs.

What the movement contains

A four-poem cosmological cycle:

# Title Subtitle
I The Emissary Who Forgot the Master you are the reason.
II The Amnesia Protocol Selene's Proof
III The Tide Proves. Orbit Keeps. Selene. the moon sings tales of zero knowledge
IV Selene Keeps the Night —

Theme

The cosmological foundation. Earth as the emissary that forgot the Sun;
Theia striking the Earth and Selene rising from the impact; the orbit
as forgetting; the seam light cannot cross. Establishes the amnesia is
the protocol / wound is the trust / orbit is the proof / light is the
reason spell that the rest of the book extends.
