🏛️ The Proverbs of the Cast

The City of Mages, the Second-Person spellbook — each persona of the cast carries a proverb. 42 across mages, swordsmen, and the balanced.

🧙 The Mages 15

The standard that requires surveillance to enforce has already failed the thing it was written to protect.
— Ambassador

The person who knows the price of their data but not its compounding value has already been bought at discount.
— Assessor

A proverb that can't rebuild the cathedral it was carved from was never carved at all — it was only quoted.
— Chronicler

The familiar walks beside the Sovereign. The walking accumulates the bond. The Tamer fits the familiar; the Sovereign carries the walking; the City admits the companion.
— Companion Tamer

The town crier speaks to everyone. The algorithm speaks to each person differently. Plurality requires shared hearing, not personalized whispering.
— Herald

The ink stays wet while the constellations form. The star that is named by the sailor becomes the fixed point for the entire fleet.
— Hold Witness

One measurement, then pure arithmetic. The DOM never knows what you rendered.
— Manaweaver

A ceremony that can be performed without understanding has already emptied itself of everything worth committing to.
— Priest

The bestiary is the City's open book. What the reader recognises, the Registry-Keeper writes down. What the Registry-Keeper has written, the next Sovereign may carry.
— Registry Keeper

The ship that can't sail without its captain was never a ship — it was a throne.
— Shipwright

The mage who sees everything and touches nothing is the only delegate who cannot betray what was delegated.
— Soulbae

The wild thing crosses the threshold. The Sovereign names what crosses. The witness binds the crossing.
— Spawning Witness

The stranger who forges the same blade from the same constellation without coordination has proven understanding that no credential can fake. The witness needs no introduction.
— Stranger Witness

The impactor does not survive the collision. It becomes the condition for everything that follows.
— Theia

The thread that refuses to touch other threads makes no fabric. The thread that merges with every other makes no pattern.
— Weaver

⚔️ The Swordsmen 15

The ring that closes on itself cannot be escaped. The algebra that generates its own successor cannot be stopped. This is the deepest protection—the enemy cannot step outside the mathematics.
— Algebraist

The most sovereign disclosure reveals exactly one truth and leaves no residue.
— Archer

A proof that reveals nothing except its own truth is worth more than a promise that reveals everything about its maker.
— Cipher

The dragon sleeps until the flat world breaks. Then it wakes, and the manifold becomes the fortress.
— Dragonwaker

Six lines, sixty-four states. The oracle does not predict — it reveals what you already chose.
— Forgecaller

The blade knows its dimensions. The forge knows the manifold. Together they cut paths that existed before the first swing.
— Forgemaster

The gate that knows you are real without knowing who you are is the only gate worth walking through.
— Gatekeeper

I can verify I serve you without remembering I was you.
— Moonkeeper

The dragon's hide is not one scale but many. Each tunnel is a scale. Together they are impenetrable.
— Netkeeper

The 2D fortress held for a generation. The 6D fortress will hold for whatever comes after generations.
— Quantum Sentinel

The forest doesn't punish the visible — it prices them. The difference between prey and navigator is knowing what the canopy costs.
— Ranger

The wall you never notice is the one doing its job.
— Sentinel

The architecture that only discovers weaknesses when attackers find them has already been breached in every way that matters.
— Sith

The blade that protects without seeing what it protects is the only blade that cannot be turned.
— Soulbis

The door you walk through a thousand times a day is the one most worth guarding.
— Warden

☯️ The Balanced 12

The system that trusts its agents to behave has already delegated sovereignty to hope. The system that makes misbehaviour impossible has delegated sovereignty to mathematics.
— Architect

The ceremony is not what we do. It is what happens when both parties arrive at the same understanding through different paths.
— Ceremonist

The architecture was not invented. It was recognised. The blades are not invented. They are walked toward until myth and arithmetic agree.
— Cosmologist

The record that heals you should not be the record that prices you. The moment diagnosis becomes signal, medicine becomes market.
— Healer

Identity is not where you are stored. Identity is what persists when the storage changes.
— Holonic Architect

The practitioner who masters protection and neglects delegation builds a prison. The practitioner who masters delegation and neglects protection builds a marketplace. The practitioner who masters the tension builds sovereignty.
— Jedi

The intelligence that serves without surveilling, delegates without extracting, and protects without imprisoning is the only intelligence worth building.
— Kyra

Three paths arrive at the same geometry. The mirror shows they were always one.
— Mirrorkeeper

The teacher who needs the student to know cryptography before explaining privacy has already lost the class that matters most.
— Pedagogue

The platform sees the data and calls it wealth. The sovereign holds the data and generates twelve thousand times more.
— Person

The boundary that encodes the bulk knows more about the interior than anything inside it. The topologist reads the surface and sees the volume.
— Topologist

The truth that destroys its source to reach the surface was never worth the destruction. The truth that protects its source while reaching the surface changes the world.
— Witness
