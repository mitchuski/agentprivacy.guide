📜 The Proverbs

The headline proverbs of the grimoire — the personas’ and the master invocations. (Each spell carries its own proverb on its page.)

Faunia 🪶 — A familiar is not a tool. A familiar is a name you have given to a wild thing that has agreed to walk beside you. Faunia witnesses the agreement.

Caducea ☤ — The herald carries no permanent address. The herald carries the binding between addresses.

Pandia 🌕 — The catalog is all-bright. The Moon's daughter holds the door. Every substrate visible; every archetype legible; every spawn anchored to the City the seeker never has to remember.

Hermaion ⚚ — Each admission is a windfall. Each windfall is a gift of Hermes laid in the City's path. The bestiary holds them. The alexandrite shows whose hand the next gift will fit.

Pleione 🧭 — The star that is named by the sailor becomes the fixed point for the entire fleet.

Drake Island anchors the city; the dragon-fire warms the bonfire; the eleven workshops forge in concert; the perpendicular operator holds the gap; each Sovereign who walks lays a stone — and the City of Mages remembers the path.
