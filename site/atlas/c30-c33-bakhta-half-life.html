💡 C30-C33 · Bakhta Half-Life

concept · shared · knowledge

Trust accumulates in arcs and decays with a half-life that begins at inscription. Canonical (1.0). Witnessable acts (inscription · stake · reveal) generate trust that compounds during arcs and decays exponentially after; the half-life is the operational time-constant of the City's economy.
