💡 C45 · Four-Chain Publication Preservation

concept · shared · knowledge

Four-chain publication preserves reconstruction-resistance across artifact types. Provisional (70%). A canonical artifact published across four registers (e.g. Zcash · Ethereum · Bitcoin · Oasis) is reconstruction-resistant in a way that any single chain cannot be.
