💡 Privacy as a checkable absence

concept · shared · knowledge

The load-bearing result of the grammar workshop: privacy-as-architecture made mechanical. A privacy guarantee is a route that provably does not exist, and the mutation probe (tools/relation_check.mjs) proves it by building the claim's minimal structural negation as a twin that still passes the grammar gate, then showing the claim fails on the twin. Direction is claimed as an absence — the 2D Fortress carries {absence: Scalar Secret to Key Holder}, and the tool proves a reclaim clause would break exactly that. What the base gate alone cannot tell apart, the relation layer can: this is the difference between privacy promised and privacy structural.
