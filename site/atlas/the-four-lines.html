💡 The Four Lines

concept · shared · narrative

The canonical closing inscription of the Zero Spellbook and Celestial Dual Ceremony. Four-line invariant spanning amnesia, wound, orbit, light. Appears on the Last Page (tale 31) and in the ceremony spec. 'The amnesia is the protocol. The wound is the trust. The orbit is the proof. The light is the reason.'
