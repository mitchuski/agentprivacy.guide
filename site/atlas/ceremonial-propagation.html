💡 Ceremonial Propagation

concept · shared · knowledge

Each Sun seeds Moon ceremonies. Each Moon trains a future Sun. The ceremonies are orbital, not sequential. Propagation through forgetting: each new practitioner believes they invented it.

That belief is the proof that the ceremony worked.
