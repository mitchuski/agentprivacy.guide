🏙️ City of Mages

civic · shared · narrative

Canonical setting of Tome V (named in Act 14, founded implicitly since Act 1, founding event in Act 11 'A Bonfire Made of Dragon Fire'). Civic overlay sitting on Drake Island and on the 64-vertex lattice. Trade Quarters (9 producer shops), the Founding Bonfire, the Temple Precinct (two altars), the Sovereign's Seat (V63), and the Lattice as Street Plan. The walls are the spellbook's 'you' voice. Open by design — Tome V continues to admit acts.
