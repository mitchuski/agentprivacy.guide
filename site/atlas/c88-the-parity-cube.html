💡 C88 · The Parity Cube

concept · shared · knowledge

The stella octangula's two tetrahedra are the even/odd parity classes of the cube's vertices — the canonical seat of neg/bnot at 3-bit scale; {0,1}^6 = {0,1}^3 × {0,1}^3 gives each agent a cube, with the C85 pair map as candidate factoring. Active (30% · Run 5).
