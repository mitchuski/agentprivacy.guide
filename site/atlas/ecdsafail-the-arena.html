🚪 ecdsa.fail (the arena)

gateway · shared · knowledge

Eigen Labs' open benchmark for the cheapest reversible secp256k1 point-addition circuit — the competitive arena shor_mage works against. Its frontier is insight-limited, not compute-limited: cheap Toffoli cuts that pass a 2048-shot probe fail the 9024-shot full validation (the nonce-island mirage). C69 (held-out gate) names the trap.
