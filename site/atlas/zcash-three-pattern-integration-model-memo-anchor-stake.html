📄 Zcash Three-Pattern Integration Model (Memo · Anchor · Stake)

document · swordsman · knowledge

Three canonical patterns for grounding agentprivacy artifacts on Zcash, each suited to a different integrity claim: Memo (witnessable inscription · C40 · C41), Anchor (cloak commitment · C40 · C45), Stake (governance witnessability · C42). Generalises beyond Zcash to any dual-ledger ZK chain admitting shielded/transparent registers. Source: docs/tomes/plans/02-zcash-integration-plan.md.
