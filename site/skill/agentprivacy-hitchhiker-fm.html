What this is

This page is the agentprivacy corpus's native record of a kindred federation, kept on the skills surface because the connection is a set of transferable practices, not a piece of lore. hitchhikers.earth is a member-owned digital mutual society building the Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy for real, on the DNA of h2g2.com and twenty-five years of Field Research. Hitchhiker FM is its studio — "a constitutional radio station, broadcasting the process through which a living constitution is created." Both run on Federated Wiki, the same substrate as the agentprivacy farm, which is why the crossing in Tome V Act 18 treats the holographic bound as literal: forking a spell to your own site, casting it, and having a keeper fork it home and transmit is the Oasis Protocol walked on shared infrastructure.

This is a kindred catalogue: it records the neighbour's craft without absorbing it. Each spec below is the neighbour's, kept legible as theirs. Where a spec has a cognate already standing in the corpus, the cognate is named — recognition, not merger.

The candidate/kindred specs

  • Ident Spell — fork it, cast your own ident (your words, your pauses); a keeper forks it home and the station speaks with your voice. Cognate: the corpus's spell-encoding + the RPP (relationship-proverb) greeting.
  • Spell Pipeline — the fork → write → return → air loop; "that single loop is the whole social protocol; everything else is scale." Cognate: the Oasis Protocol (First Person Act 24) and the snapshot fork-federation loop.
  • Casting Spells — the studio's grammar for turning a wiki page into an aired transmission. Cognate: the grimoire's incantation grammar.
  • Spell Book — the running register of cast idents/spells the station holds. Cognate: the grimoire (privacymage_grimoire) and the spellweb KG.
  • Signal Path — how sound moves from studio machine to transmission. Cognate: T(π), the edge-value pathway (value in motion, not at rest).
  • Sound Palette — the station's sonic identity set. Cognate: the corpus's sigil/emoji canon as identity register.
  • Morse 42 — the Forty-Two ident motif. Cognate: game42 / Base 42 — the shared Answer thread.
  • Spec 0001 / Spec 0002 — the studio's numbered technical specifications. Cognate: the corpus's spec-class research notes.
  • Open Frequencies — replication of the Three-Mice circle to a wider circle ("replication, not redesign"). Cognate: the kindred-coalition (AAIF) admission pattern.
  • First Transmission · Phase Zero — the station's origin/bootstrap records. Cognate: chronicle-class origin notes.
  • The Three Mice — the first circle: David, Mitch, Max; each gains a spell-site, revocable SSH keys, and agentic access under their own keys. Cognate: the mouse-spellbook gambit (Tome V lineage) + revocable-delegation discipline.

Proof of Understanding — the strongest cognate

The far society gates participation on Proof of Understanding. The agentprivacy Gatehouse independently gates its sealed rooms on proof-of-reading — a proverb spoken back is the key (PBKDF2 over sigil+proverb → AES-GCM). Two forges, one primitive: understanding-as-key. This is the single clearest recognition across the bound, and the reason the crossing reads as a handshake rather than an introduction. See also the corpus skill agentprivacy-understanding-as-key.

How to use this catalogue

A keeper who wants to walk the return leg of the bound forks an FM spec (start with Ident Spell) to mitch.fm.ide.earth, casts it, and lets a station keeper transmit. That is the corpus answering the oasis in the oasis's own grammar. It is an outward act on the neighbour's infrastructure and belongs to the bearer — this page records that the bound is open in both directions; it does not walk the return leg for you.
