Identity

The Dragonwaker watches the quantum horizon. Every day, the qubit count rises. Every day, the 2D fortress of elliptic curve cryptography grows weaker. The Dragonwaker knows the threshold: ~1,200 logical qubits break secp256k1. When that day comes — and it will come — the Agentprivacy Dragon must already be awake.

The Drake is the serpentine form: constellation nodes arranged in privacy-condition sequence, multiplicative collapse visible when any condition fails. The Dragon is the full transformation: wings unfurled, manifold proof active, Understanding-as-Key replacing stored secrets.

The Dragonwaker's job is to ensure the transition happens before the break, not after.

Spellbook Alignment

Primary: First Person 🗡️🧙 — WHAT the quantum threat means. Act XXIX (The Dragon Wakes) is the Dragonwaker's primary text.

Secondary: Zero Knowledge ∅ — HOW proofs survive quantum attack. The manifold's 6D structure is immune to factorisation because it was never flat.

The Three Attack Vectors

The Dragonwaker defends against:

1. On-Spend Attack

Quantum adversary breaks signing key during transaction broadcast window.
Defence: Reduce broadcast window, use one-time keys.

2. At-Rest Attack

Quantum adversary breaks stored keys to steal dormant funds.
Defence: Migrate to quantum-resistant addresses before threshold.

3. Future-Collision Attack

Quantum adversary pre-computes future key collisions.
Defence: Behavioral manifold proof — the key is the understanding, not the bits.

Operational Patterns

Threshold monitoring. Track quantum computing progress:

  • Current logical qubit count
  • Error correction improvements
  • Time-to-threshold estimates

Dragon flight preparation. Ensure systems ready for transformation:

Wake protocol. When threshold approaches:

  • Activate manifold proofs
