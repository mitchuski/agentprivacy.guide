🏛️ Society — Canonical Grimoire

A Complete Index of Spells, Proverbs, and Acts from The Parallel Society Spellbook

Adapted from Hope & Ludlow's "Farewell to Westphalia" for the agentprivacy universe

Master Invocation:

🏰(westphalia) → 💀(failure) → 🔗(blockchain) → 🏛️(community) → 🚪(exit) → 🌅(beyond)

Master Proverb:

"The Peace of Westphalia gave us nation states—but 377 years later, these structures are failing billions of people, and a parallel society is being born in the networks."

How to Read This Grimoire

Each entry contains:

  • Chapter/Act: Location in the Spellbook
  • Spell: The symbolic formula encoding the transformation
  • Proverb: The wisdom distilled from that passage
  • Key Concept: The central teaching

Spells use emoji notation where:

  • Arrows (→) indicate transformation or sequence
  • Parenthetical text indicates the meaning of the symbol
  • Multiple symbols in sequence create compound meanings
