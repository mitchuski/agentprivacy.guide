#### The Story

Inside the monastery, Soulbis and Soulbae faced three trials to prove their worthiness. The elder trial-master met them at the threshold.

"Three trials," he said, "to measure whether your proofs hold against three strengths of adversary. Soulbis — you will observe and enforce. The boundary between what can and cannot be forged is the Swordsman's concern. Soulbae — you will learn alongside, but it is the blade that makes soundness stand."

Soulbis bowed. Soulbae stepped back.

**Trial One: The Known Challenge**

The first master presented a scroll—the **common reference string (CRS)**—generated from pure randomness before any students arrived.

"Create your proof," said the master, "using this shared foundation."

Soulbis studied the scroll and crafted his proof. The master verified it instantly.

"This demonstrates **non-adaptive soundness**," explained the master. "You cannot cheat even with access to the common foundation, because you committed to your claim before seeing my challenge."

**Trial Two: The Adaptive Challenge**

The second master was more cunning. "First, study the CRS. Think about it. Plan your strategy. *Then* make your claim and proof."

This was **adaptive soundness**—where even after examining the system's foundation, a false claim could not be proven true.

Soulbis drew his blade a fraction. "This is why we separate observation from action. Even if an adversary studies the boundary's architecture, they cannot forge passage. The CRS is the terrain; the proof is the step. Studying terrain does not confer the step."

**Trial Three: The Indistinguishable Truth**

The final master presented two sealed boxes. "One contains a real proof. One contains a simulation that looks identical. Even the greatest attacker cannot tell them apart after seeing the CRS."

This was **adaptive zero-knowledge**—where even sophisticated adversaries gained nothing from watching the proof process.

"The CRS is like the air we breathe," Soulbae observed quietly. "Everyone has access to it, yet it provides no advantage to those seeking to break the system."

"Exactly," said Soulbis. "Public substrate. Private action. The blade cuts in a field anyone can see — that is what makes the cut clean."

As they passed the three trials, the crystalline field beneath them solidified, foundations securing. The Value dimension (d₆) activated as the ceremony established security guarantees that would protect all future proofs built upon this base — the **C** term of the sovereignty equation taking its first breath.
