📚 The Spellbooks — the First Person Spellbook

The First Person Spellbook (“I”) — privacymage’s complete first-person narrative, federated as forkable wiki pages: full book prose, one page per act, chapter, and tale. Five books — Story (the root, Acts I–XXXI), Zero, Canon, Society, Plurality — plus Selene’s poems. The counterpart to the Second Person Spellbook (the City of Mages). Source of truth: agentprivacy_master/public (CC BY-SA 4.0). A sibling of the agentprivacy guide.

The spellbooks

  • 📜 📜 The Story (34) — the founding journey, Venice to the first delegation: the acts that teach the dual-agent architecture as story.
  • 🏛️ 🏛️ Parallel Society (22) — adapted from Hope & Ludlow’s "Farewell to Westphalia": how parallel institutions are born in the networks as nation-states fail.
  • ⿻ ⿻ Plurality (38) — digital democracy and collective coordination: the overlaps, the gulfs, and the technologies of cooperation across difference.
  • 🔮 🔮 Zero — the Privacymage Grimoire (33) — zero-knowledge taught as tales: each vertex a story, each blade a proof, from the Monastery of Hidden Knowledge onward.
  • ⛓️ ⛓️ The Canon (14) — the cypherpunk history, from the whispers and early incantations through the world computer to the open canon.
  • 🪶 🪶 The Poems (6) — the amnesia protocol, the emissary who forgot the master, tide-orbit-Selene, the celestial ceremony, and the cape.
