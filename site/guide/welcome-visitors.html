0xagentprivacy — The Guide

Welcome to the federated guide to agentprivacy: the dual-agent privacy framework, the skills its agents carry, the canon that grounds it, and the city it lives in. Everything here is a forkable wiki page — read it, fork it, write on it, carry it away with its lineage. The git repositories remain the source of truth; this wiki is a projection that records its origin.

The federation

The guide is the front door. Each part of the canon is its own forkable wiki site:

  • 🗃️ The Skill Library (LIVE) — 163 forkable skills — 42 personas + role / privacy-layer / meta / wikis. Fork any page to materialize a local SKILL.md.
  • 📖 The Tomes & Cast (LIVE) — the Second-Person Spellbook (59 acts, Tomes I–X — incl. IX the Horizon and X the Hearth) + cast + 12 specs — the lore that holds the system.
  • 🧮 The Research & Conjectures (LIVE) — the privacy-value model V(π,t), the conjecture register C1–C96 (one page each, incl. Band X — the Hearthold Reading), and the PVM v6 papers.
  • 🌐 The Atlas (LIVE) — the knowledge graph — 683 nodes and 1,654 edges — the backbone that hyperlinks every other site.
  • 📚 The Library (LIVE) — the five narrative spellbooks — the Story, Parallel Society, Plurality, the Privacymage Grimoire (Zero), and the blockchain Canon — full book prose, one page per act/chapter/tale.
  • 🔮 The Grimoire (LIVE) — the grimoire as a spellbook of atoms — 70 spells, 18 incantations, 17 vertices, proverbs, blades, principles, and Selene’s Spellbook poems, each forkable and cross-linked to its persona.
