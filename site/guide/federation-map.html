How they federate

Each site is a real farm host (~/.wiki/<host>/ — pages + assets/<slug>/ + status/owner.json), served by wiki --data ~/.wiki --port 3030 --farm. Pages cross-link across sites through the FedWiki neighborhood, and the cross-links mirror the spellweb knowledge graph edges (defines, proves, narrates, inhabits, kin_to, …) — so the wiki web is the canonical relationship graph.
